What is Clear Moon?

Welcome to an application that will help all astronomy fans to forcast Moon Phases within 4 days. Application pulls information with moon rises and moon sets with weather information as well as viewing times for the international space station that's based on the users location. It's time to bring awarenass to more intellectual date nights...
